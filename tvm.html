Thiruvananthapuram offers a number of places to see and things to do in to its visitors for an unforgettable Kerala holiday experience.
One can also visit the nearby Vishnu Temple here,Padmanabhaswami Temple which is believed to be about 2000 years old. Don't forget to take a dip in the water here, as it is supposed to absolve all the sins.
Ponmudi hill station includes the lush forest area and has tropical vegetation.A variety of wildlife inhabit the hills. The Golden Valley offers vistas of the hills and access to the Kallar River. Flowing through a wilderness area, it has rounded pebbles, cool water, fish, and lush green trees.
A laid-back wooden boat ride to Kovalam's Main Beach is also an ideal thing to do along with indulging in sports water sports activities like parasailing, paragliding, swimming, surfing, skiing, catamaran ride, and scuba diving among others at Kovalam Beach.
