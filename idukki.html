Munnar

A hill station and former resort for the British Raj elite, it's surrounded by rolling hills dotted with tea plantations

The thoovanam Waterfalls originating from the Pambar River, the waterfalls amid captivating surroundings offer one of the most beautiful sights of Nature.The waterfalls get a spellbinding charm during and after the monsoon season. The sight of silver water falling deep into the rocks is best during the monsoon because the monsoon rain gives more attractiveness to the waterfalls.
Periyar Wildlife Reserve is noted for herds of Asian elephants, sometimes having 50 members. In addition, bonnet monkeys, nilgai (Indian antelope), langurs, porcupines, sloth bears, tigers, leopards, barking deer, gray jungle fowl, etc. are found in the preserve.
